Euphoria Art Show






Visit our art show!


Los Angeles has emerged as a global epicenter of art & culture, with a distinct multi-cultural influence. Diversity is our strength and art is most impactful when it transcends all borders. The Euphoria Art Show continues to lead the way with innovative programming and one-of-a-kind experiences for an expanding audience.

More than 180,000 square feet of exhibition space is committed to today’s prominent galleries. These domestic and international galleries, beyond their booths, curate special exhibits that are at the forefront of the burgeoning contemporary art movement.

World class venue


The Euphoria Art Show is strategically situated at the city’s dynamic epicenter with convenient access to world-renowned LA LIVE!, home to the Grammy Awards, The Grammy Museum, and an impressive entertainment complex that includes the Nokia Theatre, the Staples Center Arena, top restaurants, and The Ritz Carlton Hotel and Residences.

Patrons of the arts gladly drive to Downtown L.A. for the best in Classical Music (Disney Hall), Theater (Mark Taper and Ahmanson), and Contemporary Art (MOCA, Art District).
